## üìù Notes

1. **NCR is special:** Metro Manila is technically composed of 16 cities and 1 municipality, but is treated as a single unit in our system.

2. **ARMM vs BARMM:** Historical data (1981-2018) uses ARMM. Data from 2019 onwards may use BARMM (Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao).

3. **Davao de Oro:** Formerly known as "Compostela Valley" until 2019.

4. **Province counts:** The Philippines officially has 81 provinces, but the exact count can vary slightly depending on when cities achieve "highly urbanized" status and separate from their provinces.
